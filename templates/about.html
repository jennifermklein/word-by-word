about

Word By Word was inspired by the improv game 'one word story.' In the game, a group tells a story one word at a time, with each person taking turns adding a single word to the thread. This site allows you to write a story with others near or far. Contribute to a story, or see what we have already written together.

I am a former lawyer and burgeoning software engineer living in Brooklyn, New York with my family. I love to solve puzzles and build interesting things. Let me know what you think.
